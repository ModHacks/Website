the biggest hardware-focused hackathon in the Pacific Northwest.

ModHacks is about the local multi-disciplinary maker community. Spend the weekend with 200 other students from all levels of expertise and areas of study and dive into the real world of hardware and 3D-printing!

With today's barrier of entry to hardware, Modhacks strives to make hardware easily accessible to allow all levels of creativity to participate in the maker community.

We want to provide the essential launchpad for designers, engineers, developers and creators to learn about and be inspired by custom hardware and 3-D printing solutions.

48 Hours of building, making, hacking, and creating.

frequently asked questions

Who can attend?

All UW students of any concentration of study are welcome!

I don’t have a team. Can I still participate?

Of course! We’ll have fun team-forming sessions to help you find an awesome group! You’re welcome to have up to four people on a team.

How will you decide between applicants?

Applicants will be chosen based on a holistic review process. Everyone is welcome to apply and attend!

What should I bring?

Your student ID, laptop, phone, chargers, any sleeping bags, pillows, and blankets you might want, and lots of excitement!

How much is this going to cost me?

ModHacks is completely free, including WiFi, food, drinks, and awesome swag :)
